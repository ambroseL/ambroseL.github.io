ARM TREASURE DATA, PRODUCT DESIGN

Data Integration Hub

Enhance marketing data management for engineers by a clear information hierarchy

Duration

6 weeks

Role

Product Design Intern

Tools

Figma, Principle

Collaborating with a PM and engineers, my job included:

  • Researched and designed the new information architecture and integration page of the integration hub
  • Delivered research findings, prototypes and high-fi specifications for product implementation
  • Validated and enhanced design details through design critiques, team presentations, and stakeholder reviews

Problem

Marketing data management is in great ambiguity

In the data integration hub of Arm Treasure Data, engineers connect and manage marketing data cohesively. While the information is growing, two problems arise in its management:

  • It is unclear to see the overall configurations
  • It is hard to track the interconnected data

Solution

Oversee the information in a clear hierarchy

With a new data dashboard and a corresponding information architecture, the redesigned data integration hub provides:

  • A clear overview of the configured information
  • An easy journey through the related data

01. Overview integrations as a whole

In the integration dashboard, users can look through the configured data authentications and sources under each type of data integrations.

02. Navigate integrations within and across categories

Using the links and the breadcrumb, users can easily check data authentication and source details under each data connector without losing the whole picture.

Impact

Winning great approval from users, PMs, and engineers, my design helped the project win higher priorities than before for its relatively low cost and high return on user experience and business values. In the latest beta testing, it was proved that my work saved data engineers’ 20% time in integration management.

PROBLEM FRAMING

The information hierarchy is unclear and disconnected

I further synthesized the problems as:



Problem 1: Unclear views of the overall data condition

While data authentications and data sources were displayed in the system, data engineers couldn’t see clearly their data connectors. Without this big picture, they could hardly assess the overall data condition.


Problem 2: Hidden interconnections between data integrations

In the current system structure, the connections between data connectors, authentications, and sources are separate and not self-evident. Data engineers had to dive deep into the integration details and associated them by themselves.
