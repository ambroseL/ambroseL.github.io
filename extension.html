ARM TREASURE DATA

Customer Profile Chrome Extension

Provide lightweight customer profiles for marketing and customer service

Arm Treasure Data is an enterprise SaaS integrating and analyzing customer data for business insights. In this project, I redesigned its customer profiles in a new Chrome extension and worked closely with a design manager, PMs, sales and developers. My final design was used as a sales demo, enhancing vendor purchase orders by 15%.

Duration

6 weeks

Role

Product Design Intern

Tools

Figma, Principle

Background

Customer profiles: a 360-degree view of customers

Enterprise businesses spend tons of money on collecting customer data. But without making sense of the information, they are just a heap of numbers. Arm Treasure Data customer profiles synthesize raw data into refined insights, uncovering what’s most important to each customer:

Target Audience

Janice and Liam, the customer-oriented marketer and customer service representative

Janice, the campaign manager, and Liam, the customer service representative, are faithful readers of customer profiles. “Know your customers” is more than a slogan but the key to their success.

Problem

Customer profiles are too heavy to digest

Having a pile of customer profiles enriches marketing and customer service, but it also becomes a burden to Janice and Liam for:

Solution

Extract targeted insights into a lightweight Chrome extension

Alternatively, the customer profile Chrome extension pictures customer data in a lightweight view. It brings benefits in:

  • Saving load time of customer profiles
  • Uncovering targeted insights under specific scenarios

01. Access specific profiles with one click

While browsing a CRM/support deck, users can highlight and right-click on a customer’s name, scan the page or search within the chrome extension to access in-depth insights ranging from the dashboard, behaviors to attributes.

02. Track related traffic on one page

When it is triggered over a campaign event page, the chrome extension can detect the campaign information automatically and consolidate corresponding customer traffic over time.

Impact

My work got great approval both internally and externally. Our VP of Product and other senior executives showed a great interest in the Chrome extension as one future direction of our CDP platform. Besides, already being used as a sales demo, the design enhanced vendor purchase orders by 15%.

PROBLEM FRAMING

The heavy profiles impede relevant insights

After converging all the findings together, I realized the core issues lied in the gap between what we presented and what our users wanted to see:



Problem 1: Inefficient data loading

More often, users only need part of the profiles to assist their job. But to access the information, they have to open the entire system. The slow loading page costs patience and trust.


Problem 2: Excessive customer information

While the customer profiles are comprehensive, they are too heavy to read in a short time. Users suffered hardship gathering their needed customer insights.

FINAL DIRECTION

Though the project is future-oriented, we expected it to be a real product soon. To balance the desirability and feasibility, we chose Chrome extension as the final direction. It has advantages in:

DESIGN FOCUS TWO

Present a lightweight view

After the integration overview was clear, two questions came to my mind about the following navigation design:

  • How and when to show the app without disturbance?
  • What forms and layouts are easy to understand?


01. How and when to show the app without disturbance

As a complimentary application, the goal of the Chrome extension is to optimize but not replace the current workflow. Therefore, the context and timing mattered to my design.

I first analyzed the potential space to display the content. The first option was chosen based on the estimation of the information load and the product goal.

Then to trigger the app, I brought up three ideas. I chose the last two as:

  • To access individual customer’s information, users can right-click the name for higher accuracy
  • To gather page-based customer traffic, users can simply click the app icon for the extension can detect the content automatically
